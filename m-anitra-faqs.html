Faq's of M-ANITRA

Different functions M-Anitra can support are as follows:

1) How to access M-Anitra?
M-Anitra is a mobile app which can be downloaded from Google Playstore.
2) How Mithun Farmers will be benefited by M-Anitra?
  • M-Anitra will help farmers in getting fair price for their Mithun
  • Provides platform for achieving traceability.
  • M-Anitra will facilitate periodic communication of scientific information on Mithun husbandry from experts.
  • M-Anitra helps to find the buyers of the Mithun through the application.
  • Monetization of livestock with the traceability also would help farmers to get access to various services such as finance, insurance etc., from formal channels.
3) How Animal Husbandry Departments and service providers will be benefited through M-Anitra?
  • Ready available data for program implementation for all stakeholders.
  • Effective implementation of disease control programs.
  • Will provide the foundation for development of organized mithun sector.
4) How to avail the above mentioned services?
  • The app is free for Farmer registration & Animal Onboarding.
  • A one time subscription fee of INR 1000/Mithun will be collected to provide the services such as Market place, Information regarding mithun production & access to finance, insurance, other services etc.
