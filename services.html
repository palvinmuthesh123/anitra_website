Farmer Tools

Price Estimation Calculator
The approximate value of the animal will be calculated based on inputs provided by the farmer It will help farmers to understand the true value of their livestock asset, which would enable to attain the better negotiating power over middlemen/trader.

Scientific Feed Calculator
Approximate feed combination including dry, green fodder & Nutritional elements including mineral, vitamins, proteins etc. This tool will help farmers to improve their livestock production through proper combination of feed.

Freight Calculator
Approximate logistics cost for species wise Livestock trading This will help farmers to understand the total cost involve in trading and able to negotiate based on that. Also it would help them to understand the near exact value of the logistics.

Muzzle api's for tracebility of animals & SaaS

Electronic Identification system (Through Muzzle Technology)
Digital tagging of livestock without any physical tags Improve access to formal finance & Insurance services & enhance the traceability. The integrity of the collected data is secured.
